PORTRAITURE
Of all the different types of art I do I enjoy portraiture the most.

Some of my portraits belong to a series while others are freelance contracts. I find the process of these types of paintings to be both meditative and challenging depending on the stage of the peice.
When I start its all about composition, layout and proportion. A familiar routine that both relaxes and focuses my mind. Once everything is in place is where, depending on the brief, I challenge myself to paint expressively. I try to bring an emotion or feeling from the portrait so that its not just about getting a likeness, but a deeper connection.

Editorial Illustration
Illustration is the starting point of my interest in digital art and media. It is where my curiosity for web design and development stemed from and where I learned my personal creative process.

Most of my illustrations are inspided by articles, books, concepts and other editorial motives. My process involves numerous rough sketches and mind maps from which several versions of the final sketch is created. I have to keep in mind what the client desires and the target audience and make sure both criteria is satisfied.
